## 📡 Bot Fleet — Projects

### 🟢 NV-01 — Nova Tiers
> `Production` · `Ticketing` · `AI` · `Minecraft`

High-throughput ticket lifecycle with role-based moderation and audit pipelines. AI auto-responder powered by **Groq Qwen 3 32B**. 4-category dropdown panel, claim/unclaim, queue management, Minecraft integration, rating system, HTML transcripts.

### 🟢 NV-02 — Application & Forms System
> `Production` · `Applications` · `Dashboard` · `OAuth`

Premium DM-based application system with multi-step interview flow. Panel deployment via dropdown or buttons. Staff review with accept/deny/trial, role management, custom DMs, cooldowns, and **full Express + EJS web dashboard** with Discord OAuth.

### 🟢 NV-03 — Ticket Support Core
> `Production` · `Support` · `Transcripts` · `Permissions`

Full support ticket infrastructure with dropdown category panel, permission-based channels, welcome embeds, claim/unclaim toggle, priority levels, private staff notes, category moves, user management, close with reason, transcripts, and blacklist.

### 🟢 NV-04 — AI Auto-Responder Engine
> `Production` · `AI` · `Automation` · `Groq`

Intelligent AI that monitors ticket channels and responds automatically. Detects staff replies and pauses, resumes after 2h of inactivity. Context-aware using ticket category and reason. Powered by **Groq Qwen 3 32B**.

### 🟢 NV-05 — Queue & Waitlist Manager
> `Production` · `Queue` · `Minecraft` · `Roles`

Region-based waitlist and testing queue with EU/NA channels. Modal collects server, IGN, and region. Auto-assigns roles, capped at 20 capacity. Tester ping on join, `/pull` and `/next` commands, auto-create tickets.

### 🟡 NV-06 — Moderation & Utility Suite
> `Standby` · `Moderation` · `Auto-mod` · `Utilities`

Comprehensive moderation toolkit with warn/kick/ban/mute/clear, auto-mod filters, raid detection, strike system. Utility commands: userinfo, serverinfo, role management, lockdown, welcome messages. Fully slash-command driven.

### 🟢 NV-07 — Custom Leveling & XP System
> `Production` · `Leveling` · `Leaderboards` · `Rank Cards`

Full-featured leveling with voice/text XP, multi-server leaderboards, prestige ranks, role rewards, XP cooldowns, boost multipliers, and activity tracking. Embed-based rank cards with progress bars.

### 🟡 NV-08 — Suggestion & Feedback Engine
> `Standby` · `Suggestions` · `Voting` · `Digests`

Community suggestion box with upvote/downvote, auto-thread creation, staff approval gate, status tracking (pending/approved/denied/implemented), anonymous submission mode, and weekly digest summaries.
